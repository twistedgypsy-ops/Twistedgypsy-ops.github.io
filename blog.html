August 12, 2025 - The Dinner Dilemma

Trying to create a healthy shopping list and place a click list order sounds simple — until you realize just how hard it is to come up with dinners that everyone in the house actually loves. I start out feeling optimistic, but halfway through I’m questioning every choice. Do I go with something new and exciting, or stick to the old favorites that I know will get eaten?

The struggle is real, but I know the effort is worth it. Healthy eating doesn’t just happen — it’s planned, shopped for, and cooked with love… even if it means I sometimes end up making two different dinners in one night.
